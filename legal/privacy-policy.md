## 1. What We Collect
- **Account data:** first name, email address, hashed password, date of account creation, and your acceptance of the Terms.
- **Profile data you choose to provide:** gender identity, pronouns, who you want to meet, household "nest" information (categories and optional counts — never children's names or details), time rhythm, city, bio, and an optional photo of yourself.
- **Activity data:** likes, notes, matches, feature usage, and Nestful+ waitlist status.
- **Technical data:** device/browser type and app diagnostics. [Add IP address, cookies, and analytics disclosures when server hosting and analytics are added.]

**Beta storage note:** during the beta, account and profile data are stored locally in your own browser. When hosted accounts launch, data will be stored with [hosting/auth provider, e.g., Supabase — U.S. region] and this policy will be updated before the change takes effect.

## 2. What We Never Collect
We do not knowingly collect **any information about children**. Profiles describe household shape in categories only; our rules prohibit children's photos, names, schools, or schedules anywhere on the Service. The Service is restricted to adults 18+. If we learn a user is under 18, we delete the account.

## 3. How We Use Data
To operate matching (including mutual-openness and gender-preference filtering), display your profile to compatible members, enforce safety rules, respond to support requests, improve the Service, and — with your consent — send launch or feature announcements. **We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their advertising.**

## 4. Sharing
Profile content you provide is visible to members whose pre-screening is mutually compatible with yours — that's the product. We share data with service providers only as needed to run the Service (hosting, email), under confidentiality obligations; and we disclose data if required by law or to protect users' safety (including child-safety referrals to law enforcement).

## 5. Retention & Deletion
Data is retained while your account is active. **Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, photo, preferences, likes, and notes**; residual copies in backups are purged within [30] days. You can delete your account in-app at any time (Edit profile → Delete my account).

## 6. Your Rights
Depending on your state or country, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to opt out of certain processing. Submit requests to support@nestfulapp.com; we will verify and respond within the time required by law. We do not discriminate against users who exercise privacy rights.

## 7. Security
Passwords are stored only as salted hashes. Access to member data is restricted. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify affected users of any breach as required by law.

## 8. Changes
Material changes will be announced in-app or by email before they take effect.
