🪺 Nestful — Privacy Policy

Beta draft. Pending final attorney review. Controller: [Nestful, LLC] · support@nestfulapp.com.

What we collect

Your account basics (first name, email, hashed password), the profile details you choose to share (gender identity, pronouns, who you want to meet, your nest's shape and optional counts, time rhythm, city, bio, optional photo of yourself), and your activity (likes, notes, waitlist status).

What we never collect

Nothing about children. Profiles describe household shape in categories only — our rules prohibit children's photos, names, schools, or schedules anywhere on Nestful. The Service is for adults 18+ only.

Beta storage

During the beta, your account and profile are stored locally in your own browser — they never leave your device. When hosted accounts launch, this policy will be updated before anything changes.

How we use it

To run matching (mutual openness and preferences), show your profile to compatible members, enforce safety rules, and improve the Service. We do not sell your personal information and do not share it for third-party advertising.

Deletion & your rights

Deleting your account (Edit profile → Delete my account) permanently removes your profile, photo, preferences, likes, and notes. Depending on where you live, you may also have rights to access, correct, or port your data — contact support@nestfulapp.com.

Security

Passwords are stored only as salted hashes. No system is perfectly secure; we'll notify affected users of any breach as required by law.
